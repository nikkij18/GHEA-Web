About

A future center rooted in education, collaboration, and innovation.

The Georgetown Center for Global Health Education Innovation will serve as a place where interdisciplinary learning, practical partnerships, and long-term engagement come together.

Mission

Advance global health education through collaboration, inclusive learning, and leadership development.

Approach

Connect research, practice, and policy in ways that support both current and future participants.

Vision

Build a lasting institution that continues the momentum of the recent conference and expands its impact.

Statement of purpose

Keeping Georgetown's global health educators at the forefront.

The center is dedicated to keeping Georgetown's global health educators aligned with global health needs and the forefront of innovation.

Primary missions

  • Building educator dialogues
  • Supporting visiting scholars, graduate students, and global fellowships
  • Providing funding for education projects that boost global training in global health
  • Supporting Georgetown overseas campuses

Georgetown Center for Global Health Education Innovation
